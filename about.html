THE FOREX MARKET

Forex exchange market is where currencies are traded, currencies are
important to most people around the world, because currencies need to
be exchanged in order to conduct foreign trades and businesses.
For example, if you are living in U.S. and want to buy Cheese from
France, you have to pay the French for the Cheese in Euros (EUR).
This means the U.S. importer would have to exchange the equivalent
value of U.S. dollars (USD) into Euros.One unique aspect of this international market is that there is no central
market place for Forex exchange. That is, currency trading is conducted
electronically, which means all the transactions occur via computer
network between traders around the world.
